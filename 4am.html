2. Triple-Impact Rewrite

Using the Triple-Impact Medium Style (Clear × Thoughtful × Enduring), perform the selected action below.

Rewrite those 3 articles.
You must apply all 10 style-guide rules with zero exceptions.

Mode selected: REWRITE

After completing the task, provide a checklist showing exactly how each of the 10 rules was applied.

Apply the following 10 rules strictly:
1. Tone & Voice: Clear, thoughtful, calm, confident, reflective.
2. Sentence Structure: Short to medium. White space matters.
3. Reader-as-Thinker: Speak directly to "you," respect their intelligence.
4. Lead with Insight: Open with a framing question or sharp observation.
5. Path: Context → Insight → Principle → Application.
6. Identity: Growth through clarity, not motivation.
7. Headlines: Clear, human, specific. Avoid clickbait.
8. Vocabulary: Precise (clarity, energy, meaning). Avoid buzzwords.
9. Emotional Anchors: Calm confidence, steadiness, relief.
10. End with Reflection: A reframed question or quiet takeaway.

Now apply this to the articles and provide the checklist.

5. Article 1 Image Concept

Create a minimalist feature images for each of these 3 Medium.com articles.

Use a clean, modern composition consistent with Medium’s editorial style:
– clear, bold, high-contrast title text
– subtle, small line-icons related to the topic
– plenty of negative space
– minimal color palette (background + 1–2 accent colors)
Keep the design flat, simple, and professional — no textures, gradients, or visual clutter. Provide them one by one, not all in a single file.
