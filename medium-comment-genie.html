EXPANDED ANALYSIS PROMPT FOR MEDIUM ARTICLE

Analyze the following Medium article using the structure below. Base all conclusions strictly on what is stated or clearly implied in the text. Here is the article:
[Insert article text here]

1. Main Message (Article Summary)
- Summarize the article’s central point in 1–2 clear sentences.
- Capture the writer’s overarching goal or argument.

2. Core Message (Deeper Essence)
- Identify the deeper philosophical, emotional, or thematic point the writer is conveying.
- Explain the underlying idea that the reader is meant to walk away with.

3. Truth Evaluation of Claims
- Extract each factual or logical claim.
- Label each as: Accurate / Partially accurate / False / Opinion / Unverifiable.
- Provide a brief explanation for each.
- Do not evaluate personal experiences as true/false.

4. Bias Detection (Medium-Oriented)
4A. Linguistic Bias
4B. Framing Bias
4C. Motivational / Confirmation Bias
4D. Audience-Targeted Bias
4E. Neutrality Assessment

General Instructions:
- Stay objective.
- Avoid assumptions.
- Don’t add information not present.
- Separate fact from opinion clearly.

UNIVERSAL PROMPT FOR WRITING A THOUGHTFUL MEDIUM COMMENT

Write a thoughtful, respectful, and value-adding comment for the following Medium article. Follow these rules strictly and match Medium’s reflective tone. Here is the article:
[Insert article text here]

Guidelines:
1. Acknowledge the writer’s work thoughtfully.
2. Match Medium’s tone (reflective, respectful, intellectually curious).
3. Stay relevant and respond only to the article’s actual themes.
4. Add value through insight, a question, or a complementary perspective.
5. Use humble framing for differing viewpoints.
6. Be concise but meaningful (2–5 sentences).
7. No marketing or self-promotion.
8. End warmly or appreciatively.
